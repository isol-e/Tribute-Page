羽生結弦

Yuzuru Hanyu is a Japanese figure skater and considered by many to be the greatest of all time. Born in Sendai, Miyagi, Japan, he is a 2-time Olympic Gold medalist, a 2-time World champion, and posseses many other achievements like the People's Honour Award.
As a 24 year old, he continues to challenge himself with intricate and complex programs, combining the highest standards of athleticism and artistry.
